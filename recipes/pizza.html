Pizza Recipe

Description

This delicious homemade pepperoni pizza features a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and perfectly crisped pepperoni slices. It is a quick and classic option for a perfect pizza night.

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Mix warm water, sugar and yeast in a small bowl. Let it sit for about 5 or 10 minutes until it becomes foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead the dought on a floured surface for 10 minutes until smooth.
  3. Place the dough in a greased bowl and cover it with a damp cloth. Leave it in a warm spot for 1 to 2 hours until it doubles in size.
  4. Preheat your oven to its highest setting (around 450-500F / 230-260C). Punch own the dough and stetch it out a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  5. Spread the tomato sauce ove the base. Add a generous layer of mozzarella cheese and arrange the pepperoni slices o top.
  6. Bake for 8 to 12 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the ceese is bubbly.
