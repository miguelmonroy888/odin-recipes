Classic Burger Recipe

Description

This classic homemade burger features a juicy, seasoned beef patty grilled to perfection. Served on a toasted bun with melted cheese, fresh lettuce, and ripe tomatoes, it is the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Lettuce and tomato
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Shape the ground beef into patties slightly larger than your buns. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat your grill or a pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook the patties for 4 to 5 minutes on one side, then flip them over.
  4. Add a slice of cheese on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking so it melts perfectly.
  5. Toast the burger buns slightly on the grill or pan.
  6. Assemble your burger by placing the patty, lettuce, tomato slices, and your favorite sauces inside the buns.
